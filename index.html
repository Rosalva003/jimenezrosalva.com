ABOUT ME

Welcome to my digital space! I'm a digital artist and creative developer. I design through drawing, photo editing, and 3D-modeling, and recently started coding to bring those visuals into interactive, living spaces online.

My work combines traditional artistic skills with modern web technologies, creating unique digital experiences that blend visual artistry with functional design.

When I'm not coding, you can find me sketching new ideas, experimenting with 3D software, or exploring the latest design trends in digital art.

— Rosalva
